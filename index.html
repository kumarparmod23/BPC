What is Ksharshutra treatment?

expand_more
Ksharshutra is a minimally invasive Ayurvedic parasurgical procedure highly effective for complex fistula-in-ano, ensuring minimal tissue damage and rapid recovery.

Is the treatment painful?

expand_more
No, the procedure is performed under local anesthesia or sedation to ensure a painless experience.

What is the recovery time?

expand_more
Most patients can return to their daily routine within 24-48 hours. It is an out-patient procedure.
